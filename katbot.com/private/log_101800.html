I was very bored today. I spent hours examining the book collection in the LeBore's living room. They have a very odd assortment of books stored there. I played a game with myself, trying to figure out which books belonged to which LeBore. Many were easy to classify. Anything with "Entomology" or "Philatelic" were obviously Mr. LeBore's. I can rightly assume books with titles such as "1001 Things to do with Yarn" and "Kraft Krazy" belong to Mrs. LeBore. I have already looked through all of the boy's books. He has a hidden stash of bomb making and anarchy promoting books in his closet. They are hidden under a stack of magazines with titles like "Jugs" and "Butt Lover". June has her books stored in her room. I have not been in her room yet. She locks the door whenever she leaves the house.
