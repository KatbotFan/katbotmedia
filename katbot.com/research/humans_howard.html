Notes:

Mr. LeBore is a very quiet, polite man. His main interests, outside of his teaching duties at Hicksville High School, are the collection of stamps and the study of insects. His students mock his eager earnestness about insects, although he never seems to notice the younger generation's sarcasm. Although I can tell June loves her father very much, she seems rather embarrassed by the fact that he is, in her estimation, a "big square".
