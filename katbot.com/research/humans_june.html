Notes:

June is by far the most interesting and perceptive of the humans under scrutiny. Even at her young age, she is capable of comprehending the fragility and hypocrisy of the world around her.

June seems to be trying to get through high school as quickly and quietly as possible so that she can move out of her parent's house. She thinks most boys are dumb, and thinks most girls are dumb about boys, so she instead spends a lot of time at her computer. While her brother uses technology to wreak havoc, June is interested in using it to communicate. She is glad her brother is gone for a year, as he is generally a "pain in her ass."

June does seem a bit suspicious about my being there. I sense she is very distrustful and suspicious by nature. Since she is viewed as the class freak (due mostly, I believe, to the fact that she doesn't like Eminem or Kid Rock), June is very wary of those around her. Being the nerdy biology teacher's daughter doesn't seem to have helped her fit in either. Not that I get the impression she wants to fit in. All she really seems to want is to be left alone.
