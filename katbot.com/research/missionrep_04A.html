Mission Report #04A


After school I intercepted a telephone call from Paula to June. It was an accident of course; I find that my antennae tend to pick up cellular and cordless phone reception when I hold my head certain angles.

Attached is a transcript of the of the conversation:

(ring ring)

(click)

June: "Hello?"

Mrs. LeBore: "Helloo!"

Paula: "Hello? June?"

Mrs. LeBore: "No, this is not June, whom may I say is calling?"

June: "Ma, I got the phone – hang up!"

Mrs. LeBore: "All right sweetie, no need to yell."

(click)

June (groans): "Ugh! What is it Paula?"

Paula: "You're never going to believe what happened. I was driving home after school today, right? And just as I was making that hairy turn downtown some old lady in a Lincoln Towncar plowed right into me! I couldn't believe it! She could barely see over the steering wheel for Christ's sake. My car is so fucked! My dad said it will be in the shop for weeks. Weeks! Weeks with out my little silver bullet! What am I going to do? Ugh! I can't imagine not having a car, having to rely on other people to drive me around like a little baby! I should just strap my self into a car seat!"

June (tersely): "I don't have a car. I'm not a baby."

Paula: "Haha, I didn't mean you were a baby, June. I'm sure your parent's will get you a car when you're old enough to drive."

June: "Uh huh."

Paula: "Anyway, the reason I called was this: Rotten Goat is playing at Starky's tonight, and I really, really want to go. Actually, I have to go. John said he might be there. Anyway, my dad won't let me borrow his car cos there's a league game that night and anyway he thinks the accident might have been my fault, even though anyone with half a brain could tell that old lady was wack. So I thought maybe, you know, we could borrow your dad's car."

June: "Are you on crack or something? My dad isn't going to let us borrow his car. I, like, don't even have a learner's permit yet, and you just totaled your car. Besides, he is obsessed with that car. You'd think he'd pick a better car to lust after. Who likes Pintos anyway??"

Paula: "Well, you know, we could borrow it with out asking, and then bring it back later. Like after they go to sleep. They'll never know."

(pause)

June: "So, basically, you want to steal my dad's car."

Paula: "Well, when you put it that way it sounds so illegal! We would just borrow it. For one night only."

June: "I don't know Paula."

Paula: "Your cute lead singer's band is opening the show..."

June: "Really?"

(click)

Mrs. LeBore: "June darling, it's time for dinner!"

June: "Mom! This is a private call! Who are you? Big Brother?!"

Mrs. LeBore: "I'm sorry sweetie, I don't like yelling up the stairs. Just come down for dinner."

(click)

June: "So what time do you think we should leave?"

Just then the transmission was interrupted by static caused by the microwave Mrs. LeBore was using to heat up frozen corn.

I must find a way to go with them.
